Smash Burgers

Description

This smash burger recipe makes super juicy burgers with crispy edges. Don't use meat that is leaner than 80/20 for best results, and make sure the meat is very cold.

Ingredients

  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 2 tablespoons butter, softened, or as needed
  • 1 pound ground chuck beef (80% lean)
  • 4 6-inch squares parchment paper
  • salt to tast
  • 4 slices American cheese
  • burger toppings of choice

Steps

  1. Preheat grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate
  2. Spread butter on the inside of the buns and toast until lightly browned
  3. Form meat into loosely packed balls. Do not pack tightly. Place ball on the hot flat-top, cover with a piece of parchment and immediately smash
  4. Grill for about 45 seconds, until the edges are dark brown and the centers are light pink. Flip over and immediately cover them with cheese. Grill an addtional 15 to 20 seconds.
